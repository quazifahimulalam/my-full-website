Our Vision

Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release injured or orphaned birds while fostering awareness and education on avian conservation.

Values

At our bird rescue center, we uphold compassion, respect for wildlife, environmental stewardship, and commitment to rehabilitation and release.

Vision

We envision a future where every rescued bird thrives, contributes to ecological balance, and helps inspire others to protect nature.
